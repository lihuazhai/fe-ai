Chapter 1 — The Beginning

It was a quiet morning when the story began. The city was still asleep, and the first light of dawn painted the rooftops in pale gold. The air carried a faint chill, whispering of the day to come.

From his window, Thomas watched the horizon. He had no idea that today would change everything.

He stepped outside, greeted by the fresh scent of wet grass. The silence of the street seemed to stretch forever, broken only by the sound of a single bird greeting the morning.

"Every journey begins with a single step," his father used to say.

And with that memory, he took his first step.
